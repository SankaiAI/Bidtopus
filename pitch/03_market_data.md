## Market Sizing

### TAM — Total Addressable Market
- Meta ecommerce ad spend: ~$40–50B/year (Advantage+ alone = $20B+)
- Bidtopus success fee at ~5% of managed spend
- **TAM = ~$2 billion**
- Source: Meta Q4 2024 earnings — https://investor.atmeta.com/investor-news/press-release-details/2025/Meta-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2024-Results/

### SAM — Serviceable Addressable Market
- 10M+ Meta advertisers → ~70% SMB → ~20% ecommerce = 1.4M ecommerce SMBs
- Target tier ($500–$50k/month): ~30% = 420,000 merchants
- ~6 contracts/year × $150 avg success fee = $900/year per merchant
- **SAM = ~$380 million/year**
- Spend data: Proven SaaS — https://proven-saas.com/benchmarks/facebook-ads-spend

### SOM — Serviceable Obtainable Market

| Timeline | Merchants | Avg annual fee | Revenue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Year 1 (post-hackathon) | 1,000 | $400 | $400K ARR |
| Year 2 | 5,000 | $600 | $3M ARR |
| Year 3 | 20,000 | $800 | $16M ARR |

Year 1 target = 0.1% of SAM. Distribution problem, not market size problem.

### Pricing vs. Alternatives
Traditional performance agencies charge 10–20% of ad spend.
Bidtopus at ~5% of spend as a success fee is **2–4x cheaper**, with a stronger
guarantee (pay-on-success vs. pay-on-effort).
